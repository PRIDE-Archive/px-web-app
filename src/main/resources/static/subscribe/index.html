How to get subscribed to the announcements of new public ProteomeXchange (PX) datasets

There are currently three ways for external users to do this:

  1. Follow the ProteomeXchange Twitter feed @proteomeXchange

  2. Using an RSS feed reader. The PX feed URL can be accessed at: https://groups.google.com/forum/feed/proteomexchange/msgs/rss_v2_0.xml

  3. Getting e-mail updates by joining the ProteomeXchange Google group

    “Choose your preferred option for receiving the e-mails with the new datasets.”
